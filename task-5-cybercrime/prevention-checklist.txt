Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

- Do not share your OTP or passwords with anyone  
- Use strong and unique passwords  
- Enable two-factor authentication  
- Always check the URL of websites carefully  
- Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links  
- Use secure and trusted networks  
- Keep your devices and software updated  
- Install antivirus protection  
- Be cautious while opening emails from unknown sources  

In case of cybercrime:
Report at: cybercrime.gov.in  
Helpline number: 1930
